How it works

U4Uc brings together innovation partners and innovation advocates to solve unique challenges. U4Uc empowers advocates experiencing unique challenges that may have gone unresolved or unrecognized. Then, it partners advocates to innovation partners to understand real challenges and team with those most aware of these challenges.

Work with an Innovation Advocate to identify a unique challenge they face.

This is a person facing unique challenges that have been overlooked or unaddressed.

Connect them with an Innovation Partner.

This is a person or company who is innovating a new solution to a challenge that is complicated by unique circumstances.
